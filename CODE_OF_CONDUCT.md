# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and PRs that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting
Please report abusive behavior to the maintainers privately.
